## Intro to HF/E (Part 1)

### Goal 1 : Describe the terms HF/E and their synonyms. (HF/E 용어 및 동의어 설명)
- field of designing work, products, and systems to fit human capabilities and limits 
- **Ergonomics(유럽)**: Ergon(Work) + Nomos(Laws),  Human Factor(북미)
- **동의어(Synonyms)**: HFE, HCD, UCD, UI, and UX

### Goal 2 : Explain the primary focus, key objectives, and overall approach of HF/E. (HF/E의 주요 초점, 핵심 목표 및 접근 방식 설명)
- **Core Philosophy**: "Fit the System/Task to the Human" — 인간의 capabilities & limitations에 맞춰 설계
- **"Fit" Concept**: system demands와 human capabilities 간 gap을 design intervention으로 최소화
- **Primary Focus**: 인간과 products, equipment, facilities, procedures, environments 간의 상호작용
- **Key Objectives (P17-18)**: 3가지 동시 최적화, trade-off 존재
  - **Safety**, **Performance**, **Satisfaction**
- **Holistic Approach**: physical, cognitive, social, organizational, environmental factors를 동시에 고려
- **Design-Driven**: 단순 assessment가 아닌 tasks, jobs, products, environments를 설계하는 응용 학문

### Goal 3 : Distinguish the three domains (physical, cognitive, organizational) defined by IEA. (IEA 정의 3가지 주요 영역 구분) 
- **Physical**: human anatomical, anthropometric, physiological, biomechanical characteristics ↔ physical activity
- **Cognitive**: mental processes — perception, memory, reasoning, motor response ↔ human-system interaction
- **Organizational**: sociotechnical systems 최적화 — organizational structures, policies, processes

### Goal 4 : Identify the scientific disciplines that contribute to HF/E and explain the interdisciplinary nature of HF/E. (HF/E 기여 학문 및 학제간 특성 설명)
- psychology, physiology, anatomy, medicine, industrial engineering, computer science, design, and management
- 단순 차용(borrow)이 아닌 **integrate** → 단일 학문으로 해결 불가능한 complex human-system problems 해결

### Goal 5 : Explain why relying solely on "common sense," "using oneself as the model", or "design guidelines" is insufficient for designing products. ("상식"이나 "자신을 모델로 삼는 것"의 불충분성 설명)
- **NOT Just "Common Sense"**: 복잡한 설계 결정은  **empirical data** 필요
- **NOT "Using Oneself as the Model"**: HF/E는 **individual differences** 명시적으로 인정 → 다양한 user population의 고유 특성 고려 필수
- **NOT Just Applying Design Guidelines**: guideline != Optimal, in real world, complex trade-offs + context-specific constraints + **iterative evaluation**

### Goal 6 : Discuss whether it is possible to enhance multiple HF/E objectives simultaneously. (다중 HF/E 목표의 동시 향상 가능성 논의)
- 결론: human well-being, system performance는 **not mutually exclusive**
  - machine/product with HF technology→ safer + easier to use + less fatigue + more satisfying
  - objectives는 서로 **correlated**
- **Trade-offs 존재**: Performance 극대화 → Safety 저해 가능. optimal balance 추구

### Goal 7 : Describe the history of HF/E (HF/E 역사)
- **Old Age (Pre~1945)**: Scientific Management (Taylor), Motion Study (Gilbreth, therbligs) → WWII에서 장비 복잡성 > 인간 한계 → "fit system to human" 패러다임 전환
- **Middle Age (1945~1990)**:
  - 1945 Eng. Psych Lab (Birth), military → space → 자동차·소비재·제약 (Growth)
  - 1980s: Computers(HW/SW) + Disasters + Litigation(OSHA 1970)
- **Modern Age (1990~)**: challenges — Safety, Productivity, Usability, Health, Happiness, Dignity

### Goal 8 : Compare Taylorism vs Sociotechnical Systems Theory
- **Taylorism**: 작업을 최소 단위로 분해, economic incentives 의존, worker = "rational economic man", social context 무시
- **Sociotechnical**: technology + social org + environment = **interconnected** → mutually compatible 필요, holistic view
- **핵심**: Taylorism = efficiency·인간은 부품 / Sociotechnical = social·human factors 포함, 전체 최적화

### Goal 9 : Key contributions of Gilbreth couple
- **Motion Study**: 작업을 기본 동작(**therbligs** = Gilbreth 역철자)으로 분해 → 비효율 동작 제거
- 장애인 workstation 설계, 병원 수술 절차 개선 (간호사→외과의 기구 직접 전달)
- **Motion Capturing System**으로 동작 기록·분석

### Goal 10 : Professional societies of HF/E
- **CIEHF** (UK, 1950) / **HFES** (US, 1957) / **IEA** (1959) / **ESK** (Korea, 1982)
